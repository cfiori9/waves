How Light Moves

NJ SLRS: MS-PS4-2 | Exploring Light Waves

1. Reflection (The Bouncers)

When light hits a smooth surface like a mirror, it bounces off. This is called Reflection.

Key Rule: The angle it hits is the same as the angle it leaves!

2. Refraction (The Speed Jumpers)

Light changes speed when it moves from one medium (material) to another—like going from air into water.

This change in speed makes the light bend. This is Refraction.

Think of a straw looking "broken" in a glass of water!

3. Absorption (The Sponges)

Sometimes, a material doesn't let light through or bounce off. It takes the energy in! This is Absorption.

Darker colors absorb more light and turn it into heat.
